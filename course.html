What you can expect from the course

I'm going to tell you to stop listening to the "marketing pros"

For the past 2 years I've been helping entrepreneurs get their first 100 customers by making them do something different. But first I'm going to ask you to STOP trying to do what the "marketing pros" are telling you to do. Stop growth hacking, stop looking at metrics, stop content marketing.

"Growth hacking" isn't going to get you your first 100 customers. Nor will Google Analytics. Or SEM, SEO, SMM, or even SMX.

Yep, not even SMX.

You haven't heard of SMX????? It's the latest cool marketing hack - Techcrunch did an article about it.

Ok, I just made up SMX. I'm also going to tell you to stop reading Techcrunch or the rest of the startup entertainment industry. It's all crap that won't help you get your first 100 customers.

I'm going to ask you do focus ruthlessly on 1 thing - being helpful

I'm going ask you to get your first 100 customers by helping people 1:1.

Your customers are not a subscriber, metric, click, visitor, or a Follower. They are people. People with problems - and the fastest way you're going to figure out what they need and get 100 of them to buy a solution is to start helping them.

This is a critical point - so I'll repeat it

I want you to STOP trying to get them to notice you and START doing anything you can do to help them.

They key to getting your first 100 customers is proving to them that you are more committed to helping them than anyone else on the planet. More committed than companies with better products and more money than you.

  • They will buy from you - even when your product isn't very good.
  • You will have a small army of fans who give you testimonials, refer people to you, and do pretty much anything to help you succeed.
